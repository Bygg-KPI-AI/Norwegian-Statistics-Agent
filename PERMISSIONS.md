# Permissions

This app may request the following permissions in Microsoft Teams and Microsoft 365 Copilot Chat.

## Requested Permissions

- Receive messages and data that you provide to the app.
- Send messages and notifications.
- Access profile information such as name, email address, company name, and preferred language.
- Read messages in the team where the app is used.
- Read messages in the current chat.
- Maintain access to the team's data.

## Why These Permissions Are Needed

These permissions allow the app to:

- Understand your requests and return relevant results.
- Respond in chat with data and status messages.
- Operate correctly in the Teams and Copilot context where it is installed.
